# PrimeRole Product Spec (LOCKED)

This document defines the locked product, UX, and content decisions for PrimeRole.
All implementation must follow this spec exactly.
Do not introduce new concepts, copy, or structure.

---

## 1. PRODUCT DEFINITION

PrimeRole is a collaboration tool where people and AI employees work side by side.

Focus:
- Collaboration, not replacement
- Calm, confident, product-first
- SMB-first, but usable by everyone

Avoid:
- Job loss language
- Hype
- Over-technical explanations

---

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE

Primary:
- SMBs
- Solopreneurs
- Small teams (1–50)

Secondary:
- Startups
- Operators
- Team leads

Tone:
- Plain, product-aware
- Mildly empathetic
- Never dramatic or salesy

---

## 3. HOMEPAGE STRUCTURE (LOCKED)

1. Hero
2. Why PrimeRole exists
3. Departments
4. How PrimeRole works
5. Platform differentiators
6. AI employees (link out)
7. Closing CTA

Content depth: balanced

---

## 4. HERO SECTION (LOCKED)

Style:
- Balanced hero (headline + explainer)
- Visual-first

Headline style:
- Neutral, confident, product-first

Explainer:
- Explains HOW it works (simple)

Hero visual:
- Conversation-led collaboration
- Humans communicate, AI employees act

CTAs:
- Primary: Start for free
- Secondary: Book a demo
- Start for free slightly more prominent

Avoid:
- Job replacement language
- Buzzwords

---

## 5. WHY PRIMEROLE EXISTS (SECTION 2)

Problem:
- Workload overload

Tone:
- Mildly empathetic
- Calm

Format:
- One strong statement
- Short list (3–4 points)

---

## 6. DEPARTMENTS (SECTION 3)

Departments:
- Sales
- Marketing
- Hiring

Each department card includes:
- Department name
- 1-line outcome
- 3 concrete outcomes
- AI employees listed as: Name — Role
- AI employee names are clickable
- CTA: Explore AI employees

No detailed descriptions here.

---

## 7. HOW PRIMEROLE WORKS (SECTION 4)

Core model:
- Humans and AI employees work in a loop

Diagram-first presentation.

4 steps (exact labels):
1. Share context
2. AI suggests actions
3. Human approval
4. Actions executed

Minimal supporting text.

---

## 8. PLATFORM DIFFERENTIATORS (SECTION 5)

Structure:
- Sequential explanation (not grid)

Order (locked):
1. Approval Queue
2. Conversational coaching (not configuration)
3. AI employee report card
4. Predictable billing (bundles, hard caps)
5. Mobile-first capture

Each:
- Short title
- One-line explanation
- Simple icon

---

## 9. AI EMPLOYEE DETAIL PAGE (LOCKED)

Primary goals (in order):
1. Clear understanding
2. Desire to hire
3. Trust

Above the fold:
- Visual-first
- AI avatar
- Name
- Role
- CTA

Section order:
1. Visual + Name + Role
2. What this AI employee does
3. Where it fits
4. Typical tasks
5. How it works (4-step loop)
6. Report card
7. CTA (reinforced)

---

## 10. DESIGN SYSTEM

Brand color:
- Primary accent: #6640FF
- Use as accent only, not large backgrounds

Visual style:
- Polished
- Design-forward
- Premium but calm

Animations:
- Subtle
- No bounce
- Framer Motion only for light transitions

---

## 11. NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

- Do not redesign
- Do not change copy intent
- Do not add features
- Follow this spec exactly
